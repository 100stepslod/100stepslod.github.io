SELECTED ITEMS

The following ten items were chosen among several candidates, as they are particularly representative of different aspects of the life of Peppino Impastato. They form an intricate relationship network with the figure of Peppino Impastato at its centre. The selection aims at providing the user with a heterogeneous collection of objects of different types.

ANALYSIS

Each item is described by the providing institution according to specific metadata standards. In this step, we identified such standards, whenever possible. If they were not specified by the data provider, adequate standards were selected based on the nature of the data.
